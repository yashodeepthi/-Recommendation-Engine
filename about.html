About Us

We are students of UMBC presenting an online rental service for potential residents who can do a complete search of available student friendly properties in and around UMBC campus. We offer a variety of search methods, ranging from price to specific amenities offered. Students can then view pictures, view floorplans, download the application, and even request additional information. On the other hand, we provide a platform for property managers to post their current vacancies.
